About This Movie:

"Alita: Battle Angel" (English Alita: Battle Angel) is a science fiction film based on Yukito Kishara's manga "Battle Angel Alita". The film tells the story of a girl, Alita, who wakes up in the world of the future without memories of her past life. She quickly discovers that she has incredible strength and combat skills that help her survive in the dangerous world of the city of Zal. She decides to use her skills to fight crime and protect her friends in the Crease. In the course of her adventures, Alita meets different characters, such as Hanzo, Hugo and Dr. Ido, each of whom has their own secrets and motives. The film "Alita: Battle Angel" is a story about self-determination, love, betrayal and the struggle for justice in a world ruled by the forces of darkness and corruption.
