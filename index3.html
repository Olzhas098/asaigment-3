1st fact:

To create the main character Alita, advanced motion capture technology was used, which allows you to transfer the acting game to a digital character.

2nd fact:

According to critics, "Alita: Battle Angel" has become one of the best manga adaptations in recent years, as well as one of the most spectacular films of 2019.

3rd fact:

The film uses numerous visual effects, including digital models of cities and transport. For example, the main city of the film, the Hall, was created by computer graphics.

4th fact:

The actors who played the main roles in the film received special training for martial arts scenes. In particular, Rosa Salazar (Elite) trained in the martial art of Wushu, and Christoph Waltz (and before) - in fencing.

5th fact:

The film was released in 3D and IMAX format, which allows viewers to immerse themselves in the atmosphere of the film even more.

The target audience of the film "Alita: Battle Angel" includes fans of science fiction, cyberpunk, fantasy and action films.
The purpose of the film is to tell the story of Alita, a cyborg martial artist who is looking for her past and her place in a world she is just beginning to understand. The film shows her adventures and battles in a grandiose world where esports combines with power battles and political intrigues.
