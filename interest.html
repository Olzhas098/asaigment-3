Overview

Alita Battle Angel is a 2019 American cyberpunk action film based on the manga series Battle Angel Alita by Yukito Kishiro. The film stars Rosa Salazar as Alita, a cyborg with no memory of her past, who becomes a skilled warrior in a futuristic city.

The film explores themes of identity, self-discovery, and the consequences of power. Alita Battle Angel was praised for its visual effects and action sequences, and has gained a dedicated fan following.

Cast

  • Rosa Salazar as Alita
  • Christoph Waltz as Dr. Dyson Ido
  • Jennifer Connelly as Chiren
  • Mahershala Ali as Vector
  • Ed Skrein as Zapan
  • Keean Johnson as Hugo
